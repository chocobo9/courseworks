This project is a graphical representation of an underwater scene, rendered using WebGL. 
The scene includs a fish,a person, rocks, swaying seaweed and ground.

Completed features:

Ground: The base layer representing the seabed.

Rocks: Two various sized rocks scattered across the seabed.

Seaweed Strands:
Comprised of 10 ellipses each.
Animated.

Fish:
fish model and its animated movements.

Person:
A character positioned within the scene ant its animation to show movement within the underwater environment.

Imcomplete feature: 

Bubbles and its animation.
